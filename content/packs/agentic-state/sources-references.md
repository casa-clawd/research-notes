## Primary Source: The Agentic State Vision Paper

**Ilves, L., Kilian, M., Parazzoli, S.M., Peixoto, T.C., & Velsberg, O. (October 2025)**
- *The Agentic State: Rethinking Government for the Era of Agentic AI*
- Global Government Technology Centre Berlin & The World Bank
- [Download PDF](https://github.com/essemmeppi/agenticstate/releases/download/v1.0.1/The.Agentic.State.-.Vision.Paper.pdf)

**Key contributions**:
- Dual meaning of "Agentic State" (governance transformation vs. psychological phenomenon)
- 12-layer framework for implementation
- 6-level autonomy spectrum (0-5)
- The paradox: Agentic State governance may create agentic state psychology
